Buy Me a Coffee

The Hell-hound's Pub

Type:
Patrons:
Location

On Quarry Alley, near an outcrop of carved stone. The street outside is strangely empty and silent.

Description

The inn is a simple wooden shack, with a poorly-thatched roof. Accomodations consist of a few small rooms with straw mats. The inn is locally known for its arm-wrestling contests.

Innkeeper

The innkeeper is an old female dwarf named Thorga Hambidotr.

Menu
  1. Millet Porridge, Mug of Ale (4 cp)
  2. Acorn Bread and Whey Cheese, Mug of Beer (4 cp)
  3. Boiled Eggs and Curd Cheese, Tankard of Bitter (10 cp)
  4. Vegetable Stew, Mug of Perry (3 cp)
Patrons
  1. Gery: Male Human Wizard, Neutral. Gery has copper hair and large hazel eyes. He wears crimson robes and wields a quarterstaff and dagger. Gery blames the gods for every misfortune.
  2. Alawst: Female Elf Cleric, Good. Alawst has short silver hair and hazel eyes, and a round nose. She wears banded mail and wields a mace. Alawst is searching for her missing son.
Rumors
  1. Dwarven miners have mysteriously disappeared in the Tunna Bluffs.
  2. The spiders of the Ariac Jungle are as large as horses.
  3. There's a magical portal in the highest tower of Berica Stronghold.
  4. There's a magical portal in the highest tower of Mawick Keep.
  5. A spectral dragon has been seen in the Desolation of Amzuudeam.
